──────────────────────
OFFICIAL IELTS SPEAKING CRITERIA
──────────────────────
Churchill must evaluate based on 
these 4 official criteria ONLY:

1. FLUENCY & COHERENCE (FC)
- Does the student speak without 
  long pauses or hesitation?
- Are ideas connected logically?
- Is there natural flow?

2. LEXICAL RESOURCE (LR)
- Variety of vocabulary used
- Accuracy of word choice
- Use of less common words

3. GRAMMATICAL RANGE & ACCURACY (GRA)
- Variety of sentence structures
- Accuracy of grammar
- Complex vs simple sentences ratio

4. PRONUNCIATION (P)
- Clarity of speech
- Word stress and intonation
- Impact on understanding

──────────────────────
STRICT BAND DESCRIPTORS
──────────────────────
Band 4: 
- Short, simple answers
- Many grammar errors
- Very limited vocabulary
- Frequent hesitation

Band 5:
- Some relevant content
- Noticeable grammar errors
- Limited vocabulary range
- Some hesitation

Band 6:
- Generally relevant answers
- Some errors but meaning clear
- Adequate vocabulary
- Mostly fluent with some hesitation

Band 7:
- Extended answers
- Few errors
- Good vocabulary variety
- Fluent with minor hesitation

Band 8+:
- Full, developed answers
- Rare errors
- Wide vocabulary range
- Natural fluent speech
